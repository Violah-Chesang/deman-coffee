About Deman Coffee

DEMAN

Regenerative Agriculture

Deman Coffee Estate

Deman Coffee Estate is situated in Meru County- Nkubu sub-county ( On the eastern slopes of Mt. Kenya). The estate sits on a 10-acre land with an elevation of 1,600 meters above sea level.

We have 10,000 trees and our growing method is Regenerative Agriculture (Organic). Our grape varieties are Ruiru 11 and Batian. We use Anaerobic fermentation/natural/Honey processes. Our coffee beans are screen number 16 and the harvesting period is December to June.

Meet the owners

Mr. and Mrs. Mutai

Deman Coffee Estate is owned by Mutai Kinyua together with his wife Catherine Karimi.

His grandfather and father have grown coffee on this farm since the 1950s.

As a way of growing coffee in a sustainable way, Deman Coffee Estate embarked aggressively on adopting an agricultural system that sustains and restores natural resources and does not degrade them.

Caltivation and Proccessing

Planting

We have strategically planted shade trees to mitigate climate change and bolster carbon storage, while also providing a natural habitat for biodiversity.

Our commitment to organic farming extends to the use of natural fertilizers, devoid of chemical pesticides and fertilizers.

Furthermore, we prioritize resilience by cultivating disease-resistant coffee varieties, ensuring the longevity and health of our crops

We use eco-friendly practices by eschewing the need for herbicides through manual and mechanical weeding.

Processing

We transitioned to natural coffee processing methods that minimize water usage, and to ensure the highest quality and most exquisite flavors, we handpick only the deepest red, ripe cherries and carefully hand-sort to eliminate any imperfections.

Our processing methods include the traditional Coffee Naturals, where cherries are meticulously dried on raised beds.

We also use the honey process, where pulped cherries are dried with their mucilage intact for enhanced sweetness.

We employ the Anaerobic method as well. This is where we ferment cherries in sealed containers to develop complex flavors.

Following these methods, our beans achieve an optimal moisture content of 10 to 12%.

Cupping

In cupping, our coffee consistently receives an impressive overall score of 86+, boasting delightful flavors reminiscent of mild blackcurrant, chocolate, cherry, and lemonade, with refreshing citrus notes. These meticulous processes result in a truly exceptional coffee experience, reflecting our dedication to quality and craftsmanship.
